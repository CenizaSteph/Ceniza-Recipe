Ramen

amen is a Japanese dish consisting of Chinese-style wheat noodles served in a flavorful broth, topped with various ingredients such as meat, vegetables, and eggs. The broth can be made from pork, chicken, or seafood, and is usually seasoned with soy sauce, miso paste, or salt, along with other seasonings such as ginger and garlic.

The noodles used in ramen are typically thin and springy, with a chewy texture that is meant to be slurped up noisily. Ramen toppings can include slices of pork (chashu), boiled eggs (ajitama), seaweed (nori), green onions (negi), bean sprouts, and bamboo shoots.

Ingredients

  • 300ml of water
  • 2-3 tbsp of soy sauce
  • 2 tsp of chicken stock powder
  • A little grated ginger and garlic
  • 1 tsp of sesame oil
  • Your desired toppings.
  • Ramen noodles (or any kind of noodles would be good too)
